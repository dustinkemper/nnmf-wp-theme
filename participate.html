Non-Profit

In alignment with the history and tradition of previous festivals, Northern Nights Music Group will provide economic support to the Mendocino and Humboldt school districts, volunteer fire departments and non-profits.

A portion of your ticket price will benefit the Support Our Schools benefiting the Southern Humboldt School Foundation.

Northern Nights is eager to work with more like-minded organizations, and we would love to hear more about your cause or project! Please contact us at info@northernnights.org and let us know more about your organization.
